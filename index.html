ABOUT

Welcome - here, all that you see is a part of my burrow.

It is a safe and cosy space that has existed for a long while, a place in which my imagination has always been able to roam freely. I like to think that each of my drawings is defined with a personality and a purpose as well as imbued with facets of others’ art and lives that I love. A few examples of the latter include Alexandre Dumas' Edmond Dantès, who has an inherent taste for the Orient; Agatha Christie's Hercule Poirot, with his near-mania for strategic simplicity; Dagobert Peche’s flair for pastels and patterns; the fruitfully frivolous work of Friedrich Ludwig von Berzeviczy-Pallavicini; the wonders of J.K. Rowling's Weasleys’ Wizard Wheezes and, not lastly, the beautiful block-printed work of Brigitte Singh. It also goes without saying that a plethora of animated films, such as Bambi (1942) and Klaus (2019), is on this list.

Collectively these form my idea of a warm, ever-present shelter that I can return to - day in and day out, no matter what - with each moment marked by the soft chinks of my many bells, each a muse, each an idea and each a spur to dream, create and illustrate.​

CONTACT

To contact me, simply send an email directly to theburrowofbells(at)gmail.com and I will be happy to answer queries and discuss ideas. My abilities range between creating patterns, invitations (for birthdays, weddings, galleries, etc.), narrative scenes, typography and more - animated or not. I am quite friendly and fangless, though I may not be able to say the same for my two little French bullies.

Tattoo tickets are also available for purchase. One (1x) ticket costs 30,- CHF (Swiss francs) and affords you the licenced use of one (1x) of my drawings on your body. Two purchased tickets - two tattoos. Three tickets - three tattoos. And so on and so forth. Just send an email telling me which illustration you would like and the transaction will be PayPal-easy.
